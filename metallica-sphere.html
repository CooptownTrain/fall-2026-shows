Important notes

Floor prices shown are starting prices from Ticketmaster's official listings. Actual GA floor (standing pit) tickets are limited and often sell at higher prices on resale.

Venetian rates click through to live booking systems with the show date pre-filled. Rates fluctuate. Saturday shows (Oct 3, 10, 17, 24, 31, Nov 7) command premium hotel rates due to weekend demand.

Vibee package is the official Metallica + Sphere bundle - includes show ticket, Venetian room, and exclusive merch. Check there first if you want everything in one transaction.

December 2026?

Metallica has no December 2026 dates at the Sphere. The residency picks back up January 28-30, 2027 and continues through March 13. If you want a December trip, Backstreet Boys are at the Sphere starting December 26 instead.
