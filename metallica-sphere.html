Pricing notes

Ticket prices are "get-in" prices (cheapest available seat) from TickPick. Floor / pit / VIP seats run $1,000-$3,000+. Saturday shows command 10-20% premium.

2-day passes are available for some weekend pairs (Oct 1+3, Oct 8+10, Oct 15+17, Oct 22+24, Oct 29+31, Nov 5+7) on TickPick - usually save 15-25% vs buying both individually.
