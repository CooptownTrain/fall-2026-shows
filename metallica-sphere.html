Honest disclosure on prices

I cannot scrape live GA-only ticket prices. Every major resale site (StubHub, SeatGeek, Vivid Seats, TickPick) blocks automated requests for inventory data. The numbers I tried to give you earlier were "get-in" prices for the cheapest seat available - which could be upper-level reserved seating, not GA.

I cannot scrape live Venetian hotel rates either. Booking.com, Expedia, Hotels.com, Kayak, and Trivago all block automated requests. The Venetian's own booking engine blocks too.

What this page does: Each show has links pre-filtered to that exact date on every platform. Click through to see the live GA price and live Venetian rate yourself. Below are reference ranges based on public reporting and earlier sales data.
